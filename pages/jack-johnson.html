Band Description

Jack Johnson is originally from Hawaii and creates music that is very down to Earth. His music makes you feel as if you are hanging out on the beach in Hawaii while sitting around a fire and listening to him perform.

His songs have very deep meanings behind them, I suggest paying close attention the lyrics of his songs. Some of his music is even philosophical, giving the listener something to think about when enjoying the songs.

  • Some of his singles include:
  • Do You Remember
  • Banana Pancakes
  • Times Like These
  • Better Together
  • Upside Down
